How It Works

From Idea to Live in Days

A streamlined, transparent process designed to get your business online fast — with zero technical headaches on your end.

01
Discovery

I start with a free consultation: Learn about your business, your goals, your target customers, and what you want your website to achieve.

02
Design Concept

I deliver an initial design concept. You review it, give feedback, and I refine it until it's exactly right for you.

03
Development

Once design is approved, I build the full site — clean code, fast loading, optimised for mobile and search engines.

04
Review & Refine

You get full access to preview the site. I make any final adjustments before launch.

05
Launch

I deploy your site to our hosting infrastructure, connect your custom domain, and your business goes live on the internet.

06
Ongoing Support

I don't disappear after launch. Monthly maintenance keeps your site fresh, fast, and performing. I'm always just a message away.

About

Crafting Clean Code, Delivering Serious Results.

The Person Behind your Design.

Hi — I'm Seb McMillan, the founder of ModernCraft Solutions. I started this business with one clear goal: to help local businesses present themselves properly online for very little cost compared to most website developers. Not a cookie-cutter template. Not a basic drag-and-drop builder. A real, custom-built digital presence that reflects the quality of your work.

I work with businesses to design, build, and launch websites tailored to their brand. I manage the entire process — from initial design through to final domain connection — so you don't have to deal with the technical side.

Alongside this, I'm currently in my third year of studying civil engineering, which shapes the way I approach my work — with structure, attention to detail, and a focus on long-term reliability. Every site I build is hosted on fast, dependable infrastructure, giving you a professional online presence you can rely on.
